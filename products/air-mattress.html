Air Mattress — Bed Sore Therapy

Our air mattress is an inflatable, alternate-therapy mattress made from medical-grade PVC. It helps redistribute pressure for patients who are bedridden for extended periods, supporting comfort and reducing the risk of pressure ulcers.
