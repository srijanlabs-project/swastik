Portable Suction Machine

Our portable suction machine combines a regulated negative-pressure system with an oil-free lubrication pump for clean, reliable operation. It includes a vacuum gauge, pressure-regulating valve, reservoir bottle and air filter, designed for caregivers and clinical settings alike.
