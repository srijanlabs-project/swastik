Fully Automatic Blood Pressure Monitor

Our fully automatic BP monitor uses the oscillometric method with internal pump inflation, a large display, and memory for two users — making it easy for households to track blood pressure over time.

What makes it reliable

  • ✓Large, easy-to-read display shows systolic, diastolic and pulse together
  • ✓Automatic cuff inflation via internal pump — no manual pumping required
  • ✓Irregular heartbeat detection flags readings that may need attention
  • ✓Stores up to 99 readings across 2 user profiles
  • ✓Cuff designed to sit comfortably above the elbow on a bare arm
  • ✓Includes guidance for correct seated measurement posture
