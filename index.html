Swastik Medica Pvt. Ltd. is a leading medical manufacturing company based in Gurugram, Haryana. We specialise in private-label wet and plastic medical products built for retail, direct-to-consumer, wholesale, institutional and international markets.

Every product — from our flagship digital thermometers to compressor nebulizers and portable suction machines — is engineered to ISO 9001 and CE standards, with full technical documentation and quality testing at every stage.
