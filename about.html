// Our Story

From a single product line to a complete healthcare range

Swastik Medica Pvt. Ltd. is a leading medical manufacturing company based in Gurugram, Haryana, India. What began with a focus on essential diagnostic devices has grown into a complete range spanning diagnostics, respiratory care, hygiene and patient care support.

We specialise in private-label wet and plastic medical products built for retail, direct-to-consumer, wholesale, institutional and international markets. That means a hospital chain, a pharmacy brand, and an individual customer can all rely on the same manufacturing standard — just packaged differently.

Every product carries our quality commitment: ISO 9001:2015 certified processes, CE-marked devices where applicable, and full technical documentation for procurement teams.
