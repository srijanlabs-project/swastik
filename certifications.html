Quality you can verify

Every Swastik Medica product is backed by recognised quality certifications and an in-house testing process — documentation is available on request for procurement and compliance teams.

Our certifications

These marks accompany our products across retail, wholesale, institutional and export channels.

ISO

ISO 9001:2015

Our manufacturing and quality management processes are certified to the ISO 9001:2015 standard, covering production, documentation and continuous improvement.

CE

CE Marking

Applicable devices carry CE marking, indicating conformity with relevant EU health, safety and environmental protection standards.

QC

In-House Quality Control

Every batch is tested in-house against defined specifications — covering accuracy, durability and safety — before it's cleared for dispatch.

How we maintain quality

A consistent process applied across every product line we manufacture.

01

Raw Material Checks

Incoming components and materials are inspected against specification before entering production.

02

In-Process Testing

Devices are tested at key stages of assembly to catch issues early, reducing defects in finished goods.

03

Final Inspection

Finished products undergo final functional and accuracy testing against the published specification sheet.

04

Batch Documentation

Each batch is recorded with traceable documentation, available to partners for compliance and audits.
